Holy Trinity's 2021 Mission Appeal

Each of you should use whatever gift you have received to serve others,
as faithful stewards of God's grace in its various forms.      1 Peter 4:10

We've just sent out letters (by US Mail and email) to start Holy Trinity's 2021 Mission Appeal. The Mission Appeal is when we ask our members and friends to fill out a Commitment Card with their pledge of financial support to Holy Trinity for the upcoming year. Note that if you lose the card sent to you, you can download the Commitment Card and print another one.

This year, as an alternative to filling out a hardcopy Commitment Card and mailing it back, we are offering a paperless option that lets you fill out and submit an online Commitment Card form. The form you submit can only be accessed by the church office account.

Please help us by responding to the 2021 Mission Appeal by December 1. Consider using the paperless option, which is easy and fast. Timely responses will let Holy Trinity develop a realistic 2021 fiscal plan and permit us to serve as faithful stewards of God's grace.
