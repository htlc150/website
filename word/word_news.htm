Holy Trinity 2020 Mission Support Lenten Appeal

For the 2020 Lenten season, Holy Trinity will be holding a special Lenten Appeal.   The proceeds of the Appeal will be used to reduce the gap in funding for Mission Support (also known as Benevolence).   Our traditional pledge for Mission Support has been 10% of our operating budget and we have always met that pledge.  This year we pledged 5%, with the intent to bring the pledge up to the full 10% as the year went on.  This pledge gap is about $12,500.  The purpose of this appeal is to reduce that gap. Mission Support is the offering that Holy Trinity gives for the broader missions of the ELCA.   Unlike most of our budget items, Mission Support is completely outward directed.  Envelopes for offerings are in the pews and at either entrance to the church.        

                         Please consider our Lenten Appeal as part of your Lenten journey.

Thrivent – Directing Your “Choice Dollars” – If you are a Thrivent member (by virtue of owning a Thrivent life insurance policy or other Thrivent financial product), you may have accumulated “Choice Dollars” over the past year. Basically, these dollars are a result of earnings achieved by Thrivent Financial that are in turn shared with Thrivent members. Members then have the option of directing their “Choice Dollars” each year to any organization that is approved by Thrivent, such as Holy Trinity. You may do so by calling Thrivent (800-847-4836) and saying “Thrivent Choice” at the prompt, or online at Thrivent.com/choicedollars. Note: If you choose the online option, you may

be prompted to enter several pieces of information on a form (such as name and

address of the recipient organization) so that Thrivent can locate the organization in

its data bank. Through experience, I have learned that the more information you enter, the less likely it is that there will be a match of the desired organization within the Thrivent data bank. So, if you want to designate Holy Trinity, all you need to do is type “Holy Trinity” in the name field, “Red Bank” in the city field, and select NJ in the state field. Leave the rest of the fields blank and simply proceed as directed on the screen. So have your Thrivent ID and policy/account number ready, and direct your Choice Dollars soon, before they expire (Choice Dollars earned in 2019 may be directed up until March 31,2020).

 

After directing your Choice Dollars to Holy Trinity as above, notify the church’s Financial Secretary and supply the date and amount of the “direction”, plus the committee/activity/organization within HTLC to which the funds are to be donated.  Questions: contact Pete Theophall at 732-542-2488.
