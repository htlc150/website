Statement of Mission

The mission of Holy Trinity Evangelical Lutheran Church is to follow Christ. We center our lives in the Word of God. Individually and collectively we are called to the work of the Gospel.

We are committed within our congregation to worship God through Word and Sacrament, prayer, study and education, witness and service.

We are committed within our community to be a Christ-centered congregation, caring and concerned, sharing God's love, and working for justice both by ourselves and with neighboring congregations, clusters, synods and agencies. We are also committed to increasing our involvement in Ecumenical efforts.

We are committed within our world to actively support mission, advocacy and service, working for justice and peaceful understanding among people.
