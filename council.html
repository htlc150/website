The Council is entrusted with the general oversight of the life and activities of this congregation, ensuring that things are done in accordance with the Word of God and the faith and practice of the Evangelical Lutheran Church in America.

The Congregation Council currently meets on the second Thursday of each month at 7:00 pm. Special meetings are scheduled as needed.
